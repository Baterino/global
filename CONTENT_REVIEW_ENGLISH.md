**Power:** ~50kW to ~5 MW based on micro-grid configuration

**OUR FOCUS:**

  - Adaptation to local regulatory and environmental conditions

**KEY ADVANTAGES:**
- Microgrids support both daily energy needs and critical services during disruptions.
  - Residential communities and villages
  - Islands and remote or off-grid locations
  - Hospitals, schools, and educational facilities
  - Water, sanitation, and essential services and community centers

**Title:** CONTAINERIZED & LARGE-SCALE STORAGE SYSTEMS (BESS)

**Description:**
- Containerized battery energy storage systems (BESS) enable higher-capacity and higher-power deployments for industrial complexes, grid-connected systems, and critical infrastructure. These systems are designed as self-contained energy infrastructure units, capable of operating in demanding outdoor and industrial environments.
- Baterino enables containerized BESS as long-term power infrastructure, with temporary installation capability.

**APPLICATIONS:**
- Industrial complexes and energy-intensive operations
- Grid-connected and utility-adjacent projects
- Renewable energy firming and integration
- Peak shaving, load management, and energy arbitrage
- Backup power for critical industrial operations
